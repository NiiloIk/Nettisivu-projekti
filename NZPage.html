New Zealand


Country so diverse and so beautiful that there's no wonder why it plants itself on the top of many traveller's to do lists. There's a little something for everyone, from the great walks to the mountains, from the hot springs to the hobbits and from the beaches to the lakes.

Auckland

If you’re after a nature-filled escape, why not try snorkelling at Goat Island Marine reserve, climbing a volcanic cone, or walking along wild black-sand beaches. And if you’re a bit of a foodie and cultural connoisseur, explore the phenomenal dining scene with award-wining wine or stop into one of our world-class galleries. There's always so much happening in Auckland from concerts to unmissable sport events, international exhibitions, festive experiences and more. Whether you’re a first-time explorer, frequent visitor, or long-time local, there is always something new to experience in Tāmaki Makaurau. Start planning your next Auckland visit with your friends and family today.
